Q: What knowledge and work background is required for the advanced class?
A: We hope students have basic Python knowledge and traditional machine learning modeling experience (such as linear regression, logistic regression, tree-based models, XGBoost, etc.).
Q: How many people are in each class? What is the class format?
A: We offer small class sizes for each session, with 5-12 new students. Most of the class time involves teachers providing individual feedback on pre-class assignments, and we send scholarships based on completion. Students who complete assignments conscientiously usually receive $50-100. Returning students join to work on projects together, usually just a few people. Teachers don't grade their assignments and there are no scholarships, but we may send project bonuses for excellent projects. All our projects are conducted according to startup standards.
