Curriculum

12 weeks, from tools to AI agents

From AI tools and deep learning foundations, all the way to NLP, GenAI systems, LLM deployment & fine-tuning, and AI Agent engineering.

Week 1

Tools & Intros

AI tooling: ChatGPT, Cursor, Cline, Claude Code, Google Colab — plus student introductions.

Week 2

Deep Learning Techniques

Deep learning models, computer vision basics, and neural network building blocks.

Week 3

NLP Foundations

Different embedding approaches and core application scenarios, such as sentiment analysis.

Week 4

Deep Dive into NLP

Transformers and attention in depth, plus the challenges and solutions around long context.

Week 5

Advanced NLP

Dive into loss functions, train your own embedding model, and learn how to distill models.

Week 6

Q&A & Project Kickoff

Assignment review and job/interview Q&A; project topics, team structure, planning and task assignment.

Week 7

Search, Ads & Recommendation

Understand search & ads applications and how they create value in core business scenarios.

Week 8

Generative AI

Production chatbots, prompt engineering, and GenAI system design, evaluation & benchmarking from basic to advanced.

Week 9

LLM Deployment & Fine-tuning

LLM deployment, supervised fine-tuning, data synthesis, and reinforcement learning.

Week 10

AI Agents

Intro and real-world Agent scenarios: MCP, Skills, Harness Engineering, Claude Code, LangGraph, and Agent evaluation.

Week 11

Project Demos

Projects run for about a month; each is supported by two mentors across brainstorming, technical support and mock sessions. A final project showcase held to industry standards — polish your work into a presentable result.

Week 12

Guest Lectures

Industry veterans share cutting-edge practices and career insights.
